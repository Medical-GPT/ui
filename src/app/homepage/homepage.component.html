Description The medical chatbot application is designed to assist individuals in managing their symptoms by gathering information about their condition and providing recommendations on what steps to take next. The AI-powered chatbot provides personalized advice on how to care for their symptoms and suggests suitable medical professionals to consult. The application's sidebar displays four models that represent the different stages of the project, providing users with an overview of the development process. With this chatbot, individuals can easily and quickly access reliable medical advice without the need to leave their home, allowing them to take proactive steps towards their health and wellbeing. Steps Pretraining

1. Pretraining

In the pretraining process for GPT models, the model is exposed to a vast collection of examples of the English language, with the goal of developing an understanding of its syntax and structure. Through this exposure, the model attempts to learn the nuances of the language and build a foundation for generating coherent and meaningful responses to new input. Essentially, pretraining enables the model to develop a contextual understanding of the English language, allowing it to generate text that is both grammatically sound and semantically coherent.

Clean GPT2

2. GPT2

After loading a pretrained GPT-2 model, its behavior can be observed as it attempts to generate text that continues or completes the provided input rather than producing definitive responses. This is a characteristic feature of a language model, which aims to generate text that is contextually appropriate and grammatically correct. Rather than providing fixed answers, a pretrained GPT-2 model utilizes its language knowledge to generate text that is coherent and relevant to the given input, providing users with flexible and natural-sounding responses.

Medical GPT2

3. Fine-tuned GPT2 model for medical Q/A

The GPT-2 model has been fine-tuned specifically for medical question-and-answer tasks by training it on a vast dataset consisting of thousands of examples. As a result, the model has learned to generate responses that are relevant and accurate in the context of medical queries. By fine-tuning the GPT-2 model, it can provide tailored responses to medical questions, leveraging its knowledge and understanding of medical terminology and concepts to generate informative and helpful answers.

Empathic GPT2

4. Fine-tuned medical GPT2 + Fine-tuned empathic GPT2

The medical GPT-2 model has been coupled with an empathetic GPT-2 model that has been fine-tuned to rewrite text in a more compassionate and understanding manner. After generating a response, the medical GPT-2 model feeds the output into the empathetic model to produce a response that is both informative and empathetic. This approach enables the model to provide human-like responses that are not only accurate but also convey a sense of empathy and understanding, which can be crucial in healthcare settings where patients may require emotional support in addition to medical advice.
